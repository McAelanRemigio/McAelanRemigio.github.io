Case study

Career Services PLM Analytics System

Building a standardized analytics workflow that consolidates PLM exports into one reporting dataset, with a data dictionary and recurring KPI reporting to support strategy across Career Services operations.

Back to projects Contact me

Problem

Career Services data lives across multiple exports, service lines, and reporting conventions. Leadership needs consistent, decision-ready reporting across appointments, events, employer engagement, platform adoption, and outcomes — without manual recombination every cycle.

Approach

  • Extract and consolidate PLM CSV exports into a single reporting-ready dataset (repeatable ETL).
  • Define standardized KPI logic and time windows (last 12 months + totals + unique counts).
  • Build a data dictionary to align definitions across teams and reduce reporting ambiguity.
  • Produce a recurring report package spanning student support, events, employer pipeline, and outcomes.

Scope

Multi-year reporting across Career Management, Industry Relations, and Operations/Infrastructure — with consistent KPI definitions and reusable reporting logic.

Highlights

  • 130K+ job postings analyzed
  • 3+ years of institutional data consolidated
  • Standardized KPI suite + reporting structure
  • Data dictionary for shared definitions

Tools

R · Excel · Data cleaning · KPI design · Reporting systems · Data dictionary design

KPI groups (report structure)

Appointments

  • Completed appointments trend (last 12 months)
  • Total students helped
  • Appointment types + totals
  • In-person vs Zoom split

Programs & Events

  • Orientations/presentations totals + attendance
  • Workshops count + attendance
  • Event totals + employer participation

Employer Development

  • Approved employers + new employer contacts (last 12 months)
  • Employer orientations vs goal + employer counts
  • Employers at events + totals

Platforms & Adoption

  • Unique logins (last 12 months)
  • Student profile completions (last 12 months)

Outcomes & Opportunities

  • Workability-IV enrollments + placements
  • Handshake postings + openings + job types
  • Aztec Mentor Program counts + matches + industry breakdown
  • Career fair check-ins + totals
